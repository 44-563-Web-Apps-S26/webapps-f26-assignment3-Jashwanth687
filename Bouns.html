Jade Bear and Jaxon

Jade Bear is a powerful hero known for strength, wisdom, and resilience. The name “Jade” represents balance and protection, while the bear symbolizes courage and raw power. His trusted sidekick, Jaxon, brings speed, intelligence, and tech skills to the team. Together, they protect the city from dangerous enemies.

Enemies

  • Chrono Viper
    • Major Power: Can rewind time by 2 minutes
    • Minor Power: Slows enemies for a few seconds
  • Shadow Moth
    • Major Power: Controls darkness and shadows
    • Minor Power: Creates swarms of glowing moths
